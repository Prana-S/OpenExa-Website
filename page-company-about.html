Our Story

After over a decade in a few of the world's largest financial institutions such as Bank of America (CM & DW), American Express (Brokerage), and Goldman Sachs (Custody Technology), Ajit became fascinated by the potential of blockchain in finance and how it can revolutionize the industry as a whole. He also realized the finance industry lacks knowledge about blockchain and that crypto's complexity is a deal-breaker. Ajit brainstormed with longtime friends John, Dipit, Tim, Victor, Abhishek, Barnet, Mike, Franky and many more from finance, technology, and academia. They zeroed in on missing onramp infrastructure, policies, compliance, and reporting tools that prevent fund managers from participating in Crypto. Their solution was a crypto margin account with onramp infrastructure, compliance, and reporting built-in. These enable fund managers to enter the crypto market as soon as needed policy changes occur and clients mandate calls for crypto in their portfolios. Once the knowledge to enter crypto disappears, if fund managers are already onramp, then a crypto margin account will achieve wonders. It will be the jet fuel for a rocket. The one idea that keeps Ajit awake at night. Ajit couldn't rest until the startup OpenEXA was born. After in-depth market research, consulting with industry experts and numerous fund managers interviews, Ajit recognized the opportunity was more enormous than he could have imagined. As OpenEXA launched, he recruited former friends and colleagues to OpenEXA as new partners in the crime.

Establishing Trust

OpenEXA is now a team of experienced finance professionals, engineers, crypto developers, and data scientists. We craft our products to the highest standard. We believe code is law, the ultimate truth, and data begets trust. Trust is the currency of engagement. We believe that exceptionally engineered smart contracts and data blocks on the blockchain, artificial intelligence, algorithmic risk, and price — not marble office buildings on Wall St — are the future cornerstones of trust.

Simplicity

Today there is a complex system of regulation, closed financial institutions, and messy crypto infrastructure, that can seem daunting to a fund manager who is trying to venture into the crypto world. Fund managers are looking for compliant alternatives and seeking tools that will help them meet their fiduciary responsibilities and gives them the ability to create differentiated products. Making evaluation simple, focused, and immediately apparent and walking through the complete onramp process and making it easy is hard work. But we believe that by sticking to this goal, we can make something extraordinary for fund managers to unlock the gains from untapped Alpha.
